About Hydra Smart

At Hydra Smart, we've revolutionized hydration tracking with our innovative universal water bottle puck. We believe you shouldn't have to give up your favorite water bottle to track your hydration.

Our slim, smart puck attaches to the base of any water bottle, creating a universal fit that transforms your existing bottle into a smart hydration device. No need to replace bottles you already love!

What truly sets us apart is our state-of-the-art algorithm that takes a holistic view of your lifestyle, activity levels, and physiological needs to provide personalized hydration recommendations that adapt over time. We seamlessly integrate with popular health apps to incorporate more data about your unique needs.

With an initial focus on supporting proper hydration during pregnancy and postpartum, we're expanding to serve all individuals with specific hydration requirements - from marathon runners to delivery drivers to busy professionals.
